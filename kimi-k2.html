Evidence Hub – 2025 Case Studies

70 % Drop in DR-Screening Turn-around

Problem: 17-hospital network in India faced a 4-month backlog of 3 000 diabetic-retinopathy cases.

Method: Deployed Azure-based CancerCenter.AI PathoCam—whole-slide AI that flags regions of interest and quantifies Gleason grade equivalents.

Agent used: PathoCam (Diagnostics / Agentic)

Outcome: Screening time ↓ from 25 min to 4 min; pathologist productivity ↑ 3×; second-opinion consults cut from days to < 2 h.

Full paper & metrics

Ambient AI Scribe Saves 66 min / Clinician / Day

Problem: AtlantiCare providers spent > 2 h/shift on documentation.

Method: Rolled-out ambient AI scribe (NVIDIA Clara + MONAI pipeline) across 250 providers; 12-month prospective study.

Agent used: Clara Holoscan Ambient Scribe (Operations / Ambiance)

Outcome: Documentation time ↓ 66 min per provider daily; provider burnout score ↓ 27 %; projected $ 3.8 M annual saving.

See Upskillist analysis

Sepsis Guard-Boost ↑ True-positive Rate 28 %

Problem: 800-bed academic hospital generated 3 200 sepsis alerts/week with 18 % false-positive rate.

Method: Added MONAI-trained temporal-CNN on vital-stream + EHR data; 3-month prospective validation.

Agent used: SepsisGuard (Clinical Decision)

Outcome: Alert burden ↓ 42 %; true-positive rate ↑ 28 %; estimated $ 1.4 M annual saving.

JAMA Summit report

AI Kidney-Transplant Rejection Predictor Beats Histology

Problem: Traditional biopsy grading misses sub-clinical rejection.

Method: Multi-institutional trial (Nature Commun 2025) using immune-marker AI on 1 847 post-transplant blood samples.

Agent used: KTR-Predict (Research / Predictive)

Outcome: AUC 0.94 vs 0.77 histology; graft loss ↓ 35 % at 24 months; personalised immunosuppression adopted in 6 centres.

Nature summary

Pharmacy Fax Chaos → 92 % Straight-Through Processing

Problem: UCSF Health received 1 200 unstructured faxed prescriptions daily.

Method: In-house NLP + Azure Form Recogniser to classify, extract and push to Epic.

Agent used: Project Cable-Car (Operations / Robotic)

Outcome: 92 % straight-through processing; pharmacist verification time ↓ from 4 min to 20 s; 1.2 FTE redeployed to patient care.

Why HealthNavi exists

We believe healthcare’s biggest bottleneck isn’t invention—it’s navigation. Thousands of proven AI tools sit on shelves while hospitals reinvent the wheel. HealthNavi is an open, vendor-neutral marketplace that lets clinicians discover, verify and deploy vetted AI agents in hours instead of months. By coupling transparent case studies, peer forums and guided learning paths we accelerate adoption, reduce risk and ultimately free clinicians to spend more time with patients.

Our north-star metric: minutes saved per clinician per day. Every listing, lesson and conversation is judged by that single yardstick.
