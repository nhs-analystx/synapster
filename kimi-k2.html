Agent used: PathoCam (Diagnostics / Agentic)

Outcome: Screening time ↓ from 25 min to 4 min; pathologist productivity ↑ 3×; second-opinion consults cut from days to < 2 h.

Why HealthNavi exists

We believe healthcare’s biggest bottleneck isn’t invention—it’s navigation. Thousands of proven AI tools sit on shelves while hospitals reinvent the wheel. HealthNavi is an open, vendor-neutral marketplace that lets clinicians discover, verify and deploy vetted AI agents in hours instead of months. By coupling transparent case studies, peer forums and guided learning paths we accelerate adoption, reduce risk and ultimately free clinicians to spend more time with patients.

Our north-star metric: minutes saved per clinician per day. Every listing, lesson and conversation is judged by that single yardstick.
